Circle4Life: A platform that aids TCSers in securing a sustainable future

The Circle4Life app is a platform for TCS Associates to gauge their own sustainability footprint, drive positive change and measure progress towards actions committed via the platform.

The application takes cognizance of the fact that a talent pool of almost 4.5 lac associates consists of users who will be at different stages of maturity as far as their understanding of sustainability is concerned.


Among other features, the app allows users to calculate their estimated carbon footprint and pledge to reduce this footprint by a defined percentage in a defined period of time.
