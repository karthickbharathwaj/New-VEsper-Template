Senci

One stop mobile application for better elderly life

Aging is not lost youth but a new stage of opportunity & strength” - Benny Friedman (Writer, Activist, Feminist)

Senci, our mobile app solution to aid better elderly living, draws inspiration from the above quote.

We look at technology as the new stage of opportunity and strength in aging.

By providing senior citizens with a user-friendly application, we are bridging a very vital need gap as far as making the process of aging a trouble-free and memorable experience.

The solution enables easy access to the following touchpoints for senior citizens:

  • - Caregivers
  • - Family Members
  • - Volunteers
  • - Corporate Partners
  • - Community Centres
  • - Care Homes Partners
