The TCS GoBrain story

In the early part of June 2020, when majority of the world continued to work from home, Digital Garage conceived a first-of-its-kind gamified initiative for TCS employees in the APAC region.

As work from home came with its fair share of pressure, TCS GoBrain was launched to beat the stress whilst allowing employees to engage in simple yet challenging mini games over a period of one week




With a new game getting unlocked every day, a cloud of excitement and eagerness loomed at the start of every new day. GoBrain became a hot destination on the web, as IP addresses and devices made their presence felt from Australia, Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia and more countries in the region.

From word searches to puzzles to memory games and quizzes, GoBrain tapped the competitive psyche of employees, as they tried to better their scores and climb up the leaderboards.


Irrespective of who won, GoBrain has set a benchmark for TCS as far as employee engagement is concerned. After a successful launch story in the APAC region, the next goal is to take the initiative global. This is just the beginning!
